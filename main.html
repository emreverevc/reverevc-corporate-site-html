Drawing from over 30 years of Venture Captial experience, Verified is Revere’s certification for emerging VC firms. Firms that have been Verified show a signfigant level of organization, transparency, and robustness, that merits a deeper dive. While not a substitute for diligence, Verified helps identify firms that could be of interest.
What does it take to get Verified?

Firms first submit information pertaining to team backgrounds, strategy, fund terms, service providers, track record, and ODD. Revere’s team combs through the materials, verifiying each is up to the industry standard.

What is the signifigance?

Revere acts as an unbiased 3rd party drawing from our years of expereinece that you can trust. Anyone can raise their own VC fund, but not everyone can get Verified by Revere.
